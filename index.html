What the portal does

Everything tied to your membership, in your account

No more paper files or in-person queues to check where your application or dues stand.

Apply Online

Submit your professional application in minutes, with your platform charge, application fee and dues calculated automatically.

Track & Pay Dues

See exactly what you owe platform, application or annual dues and upload proof of payment for the Council to verify.

Digital License

Once approved, your license number and status are issued to your account and stay up to date as you pay off your balance.

Why CMSP

Built for how the Council actually works

  • One balance, three fee typesPlatform charge, application fee and annual dues are tracked separately so you always know what's settled and what's next.
  • Human verification, and auto-approvalEvery payment proof you upload is also reviewed by a Council operator before your balance updates.
  • Works from your phoneApply, check your balance or upload proof of payment from any device, wherever you are.
